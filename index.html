Hello! Thank you for taking the time to visit my online art gallery! My name is Paige Gugeler and I am an art and science enthusiaste. Currently I am taking part in The Knowledge Societey (TKS) where I explore exponential technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Genomics and Nanotechnology. Some of my recent work includes a recommendation deck for Walmart Canada to decrease costs of operation, and one for Wealthsimple to increase Canada Learning Bond sign ups. I am also personally working on projects around artificial intelligence and releasing articles and other media for them. Another form of media i'm interested in is visual arts (hence this website). I don't have a favourite medium of art so I've included my top three in this gallery. I hope you enjoy!
